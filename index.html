The Rock Roots of Jacksonville Florida

Jacksonville Florida for several decades has served as a crossroads for aspiring and accomplished Rock Artists. Several established world famous bands such as Lynyrd Skynyrd and Molly Hatchet started in Jacksonville and others like the Allman Brothers and Dr. John made Jacksonville their home for several years on the road to the top of the charts.
